Our Story

Hi, I'm Haley

I'm a PhD candidate in biochemistry — and a baker who takes ingredients just as seriously in the kitchen as in the lab. Sugar Lab was born from the belief that the best baked goods aren't just made with love, they're made with precision. Every recipe I develop draws on a real understanding of the chemistry behind texture, structure, and flavor.

PhD candidate in biochemistry — ingredients measured to the gram

The Difference

Baking is applied chemistry

As a PhD biochemist, Haley understands flavor and texture at a molecular level — the Maillard reaction, emulsification, gluten network formation, sugar crystallization. That knowledge isn't just academic. It's in every crumb.

Good to Know

Common questions

How far in advance do I need to order?

Custom orders require a minimum of 2 weeks notice. For cakesicle flights, reach out as early as possible — availability is limited each week.

Do you deliver?

Pickup is available in Brighton. Local Boston delivery may be available for an additional fee — mention it in your order and Haley will confirm. For out of state shipping, please inquire directly.

How do I pay?

Payment is via Venmo (@HaleyCurtis11). A 50% deposit is required to secure your order, with the balance due at pickup or delivery.

Can I request a custom flavor or design?

Absolutely — custom requests are Haley's favorite part. Describe your vision in your order form and she'll work with you to make it happen.
